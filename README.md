### What is this?

Farang Kee Nok is a humorous web app that calculates **how long you can realistically survive in Thailand** based on your monthly budget and chosen lifestyle.

It's not a serious cost-of-living calculator.  
It's a meme tool with dark humor that shows you the painful truth about your future in the Land of Smiles.

### The 4 Legendary Modes

1. **Farang Bourré** 🍺🔥  
   Beer, Go-Go bars, lady drinks, Walking Street, police fines, and questionable life choices.

2. **Digital Nomad Broke** 💻🐀  
   Cheap coworking, street food, broken scooter, slow internet, and endless visa runs.

3. **Thaï Wife + Belle Famille** 👰‍♀️🏠  
   Bigger house, sin sod, endless gifts, village parties... and the legendary **"sick buffalo"** surprise expense.

4. **Survival Simulator** ⏳💀  
   Enter your monthly budget and watch the brutal countdown begin.

### Features

- Real-time budget slider (the more money you have, the more you spend — classic lifestyle inflation)
- Dynamic expenses that scale with your budget
- The infamous **"Buffle malade"** (sick buffalo) random expense in Wife Mode
- 15+ savage punchlines that get darker as your money runs out
- Beautifully trash dark design with maximum emojis
- Fully responsive (perfect for checking while drunk on your phone)

### Tech Stack

- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Pure chaos and bad decisions
